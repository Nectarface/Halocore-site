Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we get started?

For self-hosted deployments, you can be up and running within a day using our deployment scripts. For managed hosting, we typically provision within 48 hours.

Do you offer custom integrations?

Yes, our Professional and Enterprise tiers include custom integration services. We can connect HALO Core to your existing systems and workflows.

What hardware requirements are needed?

Minimum: 16GB RAM, 4-core CPU. Recommended: 64GB RAM, RTX 4090 or better GPU for model inference. We provide detailed specs based on your use case.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional tier. You can also deploy the open-source version for unlimited testing.
