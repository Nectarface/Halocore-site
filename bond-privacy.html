Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026 | Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Introduction

BOND ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using BOND, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Anonymous user identifier (Firebase Auth UID)
  • Couple connection code (if applicable)
  • Optional: Display name

User Content:

  • Messages sent to your partner
  • Photos and memories you save
  • Calendar events you create
  • Game answers and results
  • Daily question responses
  • Status updates

2. Information Automatically Collected

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers

Usage Data:

  • App features used
  • Time spent in app
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Anonymous sign-in
  • Firestore: Storing your data
  • Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Analytics: App usage (anonymous)
  • Crashlytics: Error reporting

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App: Enable messaging between partners, store your memories and events, track game scores and stats
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs and crashes, understand feature usage, develop new features
  • Communicate with you: Send push notifications (if enabled), respond to support requests

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your messages with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Allow unauthorized access to your content

We May Share:

With Your Partner:

  • All content you explicitly share in the app
  • Messages, memories, calendar events, game results

Service Providers:

  • Google Firebase (hosting and authentication)
  • Only for operational purposes

Legal Requirements:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication: Anonymous auth with secure tokens
  • Access Control: Only you and your partner can access your data
  • Firestore Security: Rules-based access control

Important: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us. See our Delete Account page for details.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent (may limit app functionality)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bond.app

Children's Privacy

BOND is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

Couple Data

Important: When you connect with a partner:

  • Both partners can see shared content
  • Either partner can delete shared content
  • Disconnecting may not delete historical data

Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • Google Firebase - Data storage and authentication
  • Google Analytics - Anonymous usage stats
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • https://policies.google.com/privacy

International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using BOND, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@bond.app
  • Website: https://halocore.ai

Data Export

You can request a copy of your data by contacting support@bond.app. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. Contact us to learn more about these rights.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • We process data based on consent and contract necessity
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at privacy@bond.app
