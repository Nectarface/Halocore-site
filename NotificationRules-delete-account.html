Delete Your Data

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to permanently delete your Notification Rules data from your device.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Fastest)

  1. 1 Open the Notification Rules app on your device
  2. 2 Go to Settings → Privacy or Data
  3. 3 Tap Clear All Data or Delete Data
  4. 4 Confirm deletion to permanently remove all app data

Immediate deletion - no waiting period

Option 2: System Settings

You can also delete all app data through your device's system settings:

  1. 1 Open Settings on your Android device
  2. 2 Go to Apps → Notification Rules
  3. 3 Tap Storage → Clear Data
  4. 4 Confirm to delete all app data permanently

What Gets Deleted

All app settings and preferences
Saved documents and files
User history and activity logs
Cached data and temporary files
All tasks, notes, and saved items

Important: This Cannot Be Undone

Data deletion is permanent. Once deleted, you cannot recover any data stored within the app.

  • • This app stores data locally on your device only
  • • No data is stored on external servers
  • • Uninstalling the app without clearing data may leave residual files
