Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the agent count?

The agent count refers to concurrently running autonomous agents. Each agent can execute tasks, use tools, and access memory. You can have more agents configured, but only the specified number will run simultaneously.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional tier. No credit card required. You can also deploy the open-source version for unlimited testing.

What kind of support do you offer?

Starter includes email support with 24-hour response time. Professional includes priority support with 4-hour response. Enterprise includes 24/7 dedicated support with a dedicated account manager.
